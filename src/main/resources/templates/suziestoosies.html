Suzie’s Toosies

For the water to flow properly down the drain you need to connect the pipe from Suzies house and the sewage system in 60 seconds.
In order to play the game, click on START. After clicking the START, the timer will start ticking.
You need to connect the pipe of the right size and shape for the water to be able to flow.
In order to connect the right pipe, you can rotate the pipes.
When the right pipes are connected within 60 seconds then the path will glow, and you WIN the game.
If you are not able to connect the pipes within 60 seconds, then the water won’t flow from Suzies house, her washroom would be blocked, and the game will End.
